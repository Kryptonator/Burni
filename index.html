What is Burni?

Burni is more than just a token. It's a promise for a deflationary future. At Burni's core is a mechanism that permanently removes tokens from circulation to potentially increase the value of the remaining tokens.

The Secret of Token Burning

Imagine tokens being burned like logs in a magical fire. They disappear forever! This process, called "token burning," reduces the total supply of Burni tokens. Fewer tokens can mean each one becomes more valuable, similar to rare collector's items.

Burni's Promise: "Blackholed"

Burni is marked as "Blackholed: YES". This means the ability to create new Burni tokens has been permanently and irrevocably removed. It's like throwing away the key to the vault! This is a strong signal of our commitment to a deflationary mechanism from the start.

What does "Blackholed" mean? When a token issuer is "blackholed," it means the issuing address has renounced its rights to mint new tokens or change token properties. This makes the maximum supply truly fixed.
